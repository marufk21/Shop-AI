# Shipping Policy

## Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed after 2 PM EST on Fridays or on weekends will be processed the following Monday. During major sales events and holidays, processing may take up to 3 business days.

## Shipping Methods

| Method | Delivery Time | Cost |
|--------|--------------|------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | $5.99 (free on orders over $50) |
| Express | 2-3 business days | $14.99 |
| Overnight | Next business day | $29.99 |

## Free Shipping

Free standard shipping is automatically applied to all orders with a subtotal of $50 or more after discounts and before tax. This is available for deliveries within the contiguous United States only.

## International Shipping

We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International delivery typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination. Rates are calculated at checkout based on package weight, dimensions, and destination. Please note that customs fees, import duties, and taxes are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in the shipping cost.

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to update after you receive the email. You can also track your order anytime by logging into your ShopAI account and visiting your order history.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost in transit or arrives damaged, please contact our support team within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and send a replacement or issue a full refund. Please retain all packaging materials and take photos of any damage to help expedite your claim.

## Address Changes

Address changes can be made within 1 hour of placing your order by contacting customer support. After this window, we cannot guarantee changes as your order may already be in processing. Please double-check your shipping address before completing checkout.
