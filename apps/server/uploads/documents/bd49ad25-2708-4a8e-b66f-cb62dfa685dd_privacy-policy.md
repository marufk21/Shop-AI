# Privacy Policy

## Information We Collect

When you use ShopAI, we collect the following types of information:

- **Account information:** Name, email address, phone number, and shipping/billing addresses provided during account creation and checkout.
- **Order information:** Products purchased, order value, payment method type (we do not store full credit card numbers — payments are processed by our PCI-compliant payment partners).
- **Browsing information:** Pages viewed, products searched, time spent on site, and items added to cart. This is collected via cookies and similar technologies.
- **Communication data:** Chat messages with our AI assistant, customer support emails, and product reviews.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

- Process and fulfill your orders
- Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
- Provide customer support through our AI assistant and human support team
- Improve our product recommendations and site experience
- Send marketing emails (only with your consent — you can unsubscribe at any time)
- Detect and prevent fraud and abuse

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

- Payment processors (to complete transactions)
- Shipping carriers (to deliver your orders)
- Cloud hosting and analytics providers (to operate our platform)

All third-party partners are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the specific service they provide to us.

## Data Retention

We retain your account and order information for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team. Some transaction records may be retained for legal and accounting purposes as required by law.

## Cookies

We use essential cookies for site functionality (cart, login session) and optional analytics cookies to understand how customers use our site. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Correct inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@shopai.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

## Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3) for all data transmission and AES-256 encryption for data at rest. Our platform undergoes regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
