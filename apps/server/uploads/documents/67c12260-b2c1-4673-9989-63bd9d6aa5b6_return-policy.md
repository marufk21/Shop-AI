# Return Policy

## Return Window

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of delivery. After 30 days, unfortunately we cannot accept returns or issue refunds. To be eligible for a return, items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging with all tags and accessories included.

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

- Gift cards and e-gift cards
- Personal care items (opened)
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Perishable goods and food items
- Digital downloads and software licenses
- Items marked as final sale or clearance

## Return Process

1. Log into your ShopAI account and navigate to your order history.
2. Select the order and items you wish to return.
3. Provide a reason for the return (this helps us improve).
4. You will receive a prepaid return shipping label via email within 24 hours.
5. Print the label, pack the items securely in the original packaging, and drop off at the designated carrier location.
6. Once we receive and inspect the return (typically 3-5 business days after delivery), we will process your refund.

## Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5-7 business days after we approve the return. Shipping charges from the original order are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (wrong item shipped, defective product). If you paid with a credit card, your bank may take an additional 3-5 business days to post the credit to your account.

## Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different size, color, or variant, please initiate a return for the original item and place a new order. This ensures the fastest delivery of your replacement.

## Defective or Incorrect Items

If you receive a defective, damaged, or incorrect item, please contact our support team within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange a prepaid return or replacement at no cost to you and ensure the correct item is shipped immediately. Photos of the issue are appreciated and help us process your claim faster.

## Price Adjustments

If an item you purchased goes on sale within 7 days of your order date, contact us for a price adjustment refund of the difference. This applies to regular-priced items only, not to flash sales or limited-time promotions.
