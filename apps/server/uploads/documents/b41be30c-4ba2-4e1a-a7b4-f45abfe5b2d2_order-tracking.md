# Order Tracking

## How to Track Your Order

There are two ways to track your ShopAI order:

### Via Email

As soon as your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with the subject line "Your ShopAI Order #XXXXX Has Shipped." This email contains your tracking number and a direct link to the carrier's tracking page. If you do not see the email, please check your spam or promotions folder.

### Via Your Account

1. Log into your ShopAI account at shopai.com/account.
2. Click on "Order History" in the left sidebar.
3. Find the order you want to track and click "View Details."
4. The tracking number and current status will be displayed under the "Shipping" section.
5. Click the tracking number to view the full tracking history on the carrier's website.

## Understanding Tracking Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Order Confirmed | Your order has been received and payment is confirmed. |
| Processing | We are preparing your items for shipment. |
| Shipped | Your package has left our warehouse and is with the carrier. |
| In Transit | Your package is moving through the carrier network. |
| Out for Delivery | The package is on the delivery vehicle and will arrive today. |
| Delivered | The package has been delivered to the shipping address. |
| Exception | There is a delay or issue — check the details or contact support. |

## Tracking Not Updating

Tracking information can take up to 24 hours to appear after you receive your shipping confirmation email. If your tracking has not updated for more than 3 business days, please contact our support team with your order number and we will investigate with the carrier.

## Missing Package Marked as Delivered

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you cannot find the package:

1. Check around your property — porches, side doors, garages, mailrooms, and with neighbors.
2. Wait 24 hours — carriers sometimes mark packages as delivered slightly before the actual drop-off.
3. If the package is still missing after 24 hours, contact our support team. We will file a claim with the carrier and send a replacement or issue a refund.

## Order Issues

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window or you receive the wrong items, contact support immediately at support@shopai.com or through the chat widget on our website. Please have your order number ready for faster service.
