# Gift Wrapping

## Gift Wrapping Service

Yes, we offer gift wrapping on most items. During checkout, you will see a "Gift Wrapping" option in the order summary section. Simply check the box to add gift wrapping to your order.

## Pricing

Gift wrapping costs $4.99 per item. If you are ordering multiple items and want them wrapped individually, the fee applies to each item. If you want multiple items grouped into a single gift package, please add a note during checkout and we will accommodate at the flat $4.99 rate.

## What to Expect

Each gift-wrapped item includes:

- Premium matte-finish wrapping paper in your choice of design (select from 6 seasonal options during checkout)
- Coordinating satin ribbon
- A handwritten gift tag with your personalized message (up to 200 characters)
- Items placed in a protective outer box to keep the wrapping pristine during shipping

## Gift Receipts

Every gift-wrapped order automatically includes a gift receipt instead of a standard receipt. The gift receipt shows the item details without prices, allowing the recipient to exchange or return the item if needed without seeing the cost.

## Gift Messages

You can add a personalized gift message during checkout. Messages are handwritten on a premium card and placed inside the package. Maximum length is 200 characters. Emojis are not supported for handwritten messages — please use text only.

## Multiple Shipping Addresses

If you are purchasing gifts for multiple recipients, you can ship to different addresses within the same order. During checkout, select "Ship to Multiple Addresses" and assign items and gift wrapping preferences to each address individually.

## Corporate and Bulk Gifts

For corporate gifts or orders of 10 or more gift-wrapped items, please contact our business sales team at corporate@shopai.com for special pricing, custom branding options, and bulk gift-wrapping discounts.

## Holiday Cutoff Dates

During the holiday season (November-December), we publish gift-order cutoff dates to ensure delivery by December 24th. These dates vary by shipping method and destination. Check our website in early November for the current year's holiday shipping calendar.
